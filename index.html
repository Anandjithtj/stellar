THE
MOON

Earth's Moon is the brightest and largest object

in our night sky. The Moon makes Earth a more livable

planet by moderating our home planet's wobble on its axis,

leading to a relatively stable climate. It also causes tides,

creating a rhythm that has guided humans for thousands of years.

HUMAN MADE

An artificial satellite is an object

that people have made and launched into orbit

using rockets. There are currently over 3,000

active satellites orbiting the Earth. The size,

altitude, and design of a satellite depend on its purpose.
